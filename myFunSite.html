About Us

We Leave A Memorable Experience For U

Elm Time is a website founded in 2022 that features a “curated list of the most exciting events, things to do and to see in your city, tailored just for you.” Our site is connected to over 30 of the world’s largest cities such as London, New York, Madrid, Paris and Los Angeles. Free and paid experiences are listed, including restaurants, concerts, nightlife and fashion shows. Oftentimes, very inexpensive tickets can be found on the site. Sometimes Elm Time will even list tickets for free events.
